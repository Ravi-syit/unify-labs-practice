Frequently Asked Questions

Registration Questions

How do I register for DevConf 2026?
You can register using our online registration form.
Is there an early bird discount?
Yes! Early bird registration is available until March 15, 2026.
Can I register on the day of the event?
Yes, but availability is limited and online pre-registration is recommended.

Event Details

Where is DevConf 2026 held?
The conference takes place at the Tech Convention Center, San Francisco, CA.
What are the conference dates?
April 20–22, 2026.
Will sessions be recorded?
Yes, all sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees.

Accommodation

Are there recommended hotels?
Yes, check our accommodation guide for partner hotels with discounted rates.
Is parking available at the venue?
Yes, onsite parking is available for a fee.

Technical Requirements

Do I need to bring my own laptop?
Yes, some workshops require personal devices. Wi-Fi will be available.
Are power outlets provided?
Yes, outlets are available at workshop tables and seating areas.

Networking

Will there be networking events?
Yes, there will be meetups, lunches, and an evening reception for all attendees.
How can I connect with speakers?
Speakers’ contact information will be shared via the conference app.
